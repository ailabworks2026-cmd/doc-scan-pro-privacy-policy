Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (used for account creation, login, and account recovery)
  • Account authentication data

2. Camera and Document Data

To provide our core scanning functionality, we access:

  • Your device camera (only when you actively use the scan feature)
  • Images you capture or import for scanning
  • Documents you create, edit, rotate, or organize within the App

3. Document Content and Cloud Storage

  • Scanned documents and the text extracted from them via OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
  • Documents are stored on our secure cloud servers to enable cross-device sync and backup
  • Folder structures and document organization data
  • Document metadata (creation date, file size, page count)

4. Subscription and Payment Information

  • Subscription tier and billing status (Free, Pro, or Business)
  • Payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing and RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your credit card or payment details — these are managed by Google Play and RevenueCat in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data and crash logs, used to improve stability and performance
  • General usage analytics (features used, frequency of use)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain core scanning, OCR, and document management features
  • Sync your documents securely across your devices via cloud storage
  • Authenticate your account and protect against unauthorized access
  • Process subscription payments and manage your Pro or Business tier access
  • Send important service-related communications (e.g., account verification, security alerts)
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance and reliability
  • Respond to customer support requests

We do not sell your personal information or document content to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your documents are stored on encrypted cloud servers
  • We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only, for legitimate operational purposes
  • While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated documents
  • Export your documents before account deletion
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the support email below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe, consistent with applicable law.

Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or by contacting our support team. Upon deletion, your documents and personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and documents for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be deleted from our active systems within 30 days, though residual copies may persist in encrypted backups for a limited period before being purged.

Children's Privacy

Doc Scan Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own. Where this occurs, we take steps to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of material changes via the App or by email, and update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
